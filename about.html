Curious about where I've worked?

Like many I started out as a print designer, adventually going back to school to focus on digital design at Seattle Central Academy. From there I worked for a diverse selection of companys in the Seattle gaining experience in user experience. I have also worked as a care giver, art installer, exhibition designer, and sushi delivery driver. I guess you start somewhere.

PicMonkey

June 2015 - Oct 2017

At PicMonkey, I worked as a UX Designer on the mobile team. I started out in a support role and when I left I was the lead designer for mobile. Where I grew the most at PicMonkey was diving into project management, assisting product to clarify the long term roadmap, and establishing processes to be more efficient and improve communication.

  • Set priorities for the mobile design team, working with product and  engineering to stay on track
  • Helped product define and create the product roadmap for the mobile app
  • Led the design of the mobile app, and built comprehensive specs for cross- team communication
  • Supported and mentored other designers on the mobile team
  • Made design a company wide effort, and brought transparency into the design process
  • Created and lead usability studies to discover how to improve the product

Getty Images

Aug 2012 - Jan 2015

As a UX Designer at Getty Images I was able to work on a variety of products from mobile, customer facing, and internal tools. I advocated for collaboration, bringing stakeholders into all design phases from early dev-design working sessions, to product-design dualy observing usability sessions. There was no whiteboard left uncovered, and talking out design problems was the name of the game.

  • Lead UX Designer for image.net, Media Manager, and iStock iPad App  project
  • Conducted and lead user testing and research for image.net by Getty Images and other products
  • Developed responsive design solutions for several areas on Gettyimages.com
  • Created weekly working sessions where developers, designers, and product managers brainstormed and collaborated on feature solutions
  • Worked to create a team centered atmosphere, by setting up multiple team  events and weekly lunches, and in addition coordinated workshops for team  members to learn new skills

MSN Mobile

Dec 2010 - Dec 2011

As a Product Designer on the MSN Mobile Experience Team at Microsoft, I had an uniqe oportunity to design for web, before responsive and breakpoints were common place. I learned to ask the right questions before diving into a problem and work in tandem with development to find a middle ground.

  • Lead designer for the MSN Mobile Weather Channel. Worked on project  from concept development through to the final product, working closely with  development team
  • Designer for MSN Video, Entertainment, Autos, and FOX Sports mobile  websites
  • Tested and designed across multiple mobile devices (iOS, Android, Windows  Phone)
