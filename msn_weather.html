MSN Weather Channel

Mobile Website Design | UX design

I Worked as the lead UX designer to develop a lightweight Weather Channel that quickly allows the user to view current forecast, hourly conditions, and extended forecast. Features include a current conditions info panel with a background image and icon that changes depending on the condition, a swipe and flick 24 hour slider, and the extended 10-day forecast.

Hand made by Marta Sivertsen
