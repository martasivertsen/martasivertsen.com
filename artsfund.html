ArtsFund + Getty Images

Fundraising Campaign . Visual Design . 2014

While working at Getty Images I worked with a volunteer group that put on an internal fundraising campaign for the Seattle non-profit, ArtsFund. As Getty Images represents many photographers, ArtsFund was a perfect organization for us to support. I was in charge of creating the message, theme, and all marketing materials.

ArtsFund has a key role in supporting the arts community in Seattle. Getty Images wanted to get employees excited about supportting the arts and give back to the community. I was a designer on the fundraising commitee and voluteered to create all marketing and printed event materials. Getty Images and ArtsFund were perfect paring for launching an interval fundraising campaign to connect and give back to the Seattle community. Also a lover of art I saw unique opportunity to be a supporter and volunteered to design and create all marketing materials for the event. While an UX Designer at Getty Images, I volunteered to lead the design effort for an employee fundraising compaign for ArtsFund.Getty Images selected ArtsFund as a local organization to give back to because Getty Images as an organization is built of award winning photographt and artists.

Art surrounds us

When you work at Getty Images you start to forget about the stunning collection of photography that you work with every day. The major focus of this fundraising campaign was to remind people that art really does surround us. I hand-selected a spectrum of colorful, textural photographs that capture art in progress.

showcase the deep archive of beautiful, interesting, and life capturing .  e wanted to reemphasize to employees at Getty Images the diverse, inspiring spectrum of photography we that as a collective we are supporting and distributing throuhg the world. Art is always around us and is really part of our everyday.

It was important to help remind Getty Images employees how much they are surrounded by award winning photography and art. As a result we decided to showcase a unique collection of images from Getty Images library throughout all of the marketing materials. And Getty Images is key player in capturing art around the world. How do we get Getty Images employees enganged in giving back to the community. Remind folks that we are surrounded by award winning photography and art everyday. Showcase within the company the very work that we promote.

Promoting the fundraiser

I created invites, posters, and digital media which I distributed throughout the office to promote the fundraiser. All materials emphasized the theme, life with art
When designing the promotional materials for the fundraiser, I featured the selected images, highlighting the theme of "life with art"

In order to promote the fundraiser, I featured the selected images on invites, posters, and digital media throughout the office, emphasizing life with art.

The final designs used imagery from the Getty Images library paired with the event tagline, “Life with Art”. The designed pieces included invitations, custom printed art-quote fortune cookies, and a series of slide presentations.

Creative fortunes

In addition to the marketing materials, I included custom ordered fortune cookies, produced locally in the International District, printed with short quotes by famous artists.
The final designs used imagery from the Getty Images library paired with the event tagline, “Life with Art”. The designed pieces included invitations, custom printed art-quote fortune cookies, and a series of slide presentations.

Day-of promotionals

On the day of the fundraiser we took over office digital displays with information about the fundraiser and ArtsFund. The displays provided a quick snapshot about ArtsFund, and how prolific and important it is in the community.

Key take aways

The campaign was so successful that it raised twice the funds from the previous year, and was ArtsFund's most successful internal employee fundraising campaign so far. They asked to use the visual materials as an example to other companies conducting similar campaigns.
